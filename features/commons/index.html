Features in the House of Commons scope

The House of Commons scope has the following features defined. These are available to assign to rulesets to build a view of answerability of the competencies. Available rulesets are listed on the front page of this site

commons.unique_members_identifier

Unique identifier of the members who have submitted Category 1: Employment and earnings declarations

commons.members_name

Name [in capitals]

commons.members_name_crossref

A theoretical cross match/lookup of the member against a known list.
